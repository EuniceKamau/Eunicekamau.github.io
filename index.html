    resource management system in excel

    The project involves creating a VBA macro to automate yearly work allocation for employees, handling various conditions such as weekdays, weekends, holidays, and employee leave. The macro is part of a larger effort to automate staff scheduling across different work locations and modes, such as working from the office, working from home, or being off.

    Key Features of the VBA Macro:

  • Date Iteration: The macro loops through a set of columns representing dates in an Excel sheet to automate assignments for each day of the year.
  • Date Validation: The macro ensures that only valid dates are processed by checking if the cells contain valid date values.
  • Weekday Assignments: For weekdays (Monday to Friday), it calls a subroutine (AssignWeekdayWork) to allocate employees to work from either the office (HQ or Gath) or from home, considering that only one employee works from home per day.
  • Saturday Assignments: For Saturdays, it calls a different subroutine (AssignSaturdayWork) to assign one employee to work, ensuring that each employee only works on one Saturday per month. The macro also cross-references a holiday sheet and a leave sheet to ensure that employees
  • Leave and Holidays: are not scheduled to work on official holidays or during their leave days.
  • Balanced Work Allocation: The macro ensures that the work allocation is balanced between the HQ and Gath locations and that all conditions for work-from-home, weekends, and off days are met.
    • Main Procedures:
  • AssignWeekdayWork: Assigns employees to work on weekdays while checking for holidays, leave, and ensuring balanced work distribution.
  • AssignSaturdayWork: Assigns one employee to work on Saturday once a month, considering leave and holidays.
    • Goals of the Project:
  • Efficiency: Reduce manual scheduling efforts by automating the entire yearly allocation process.
  • Fairness: Ensure that work-from-home and Saturday work assignments are distributed fairly among employees.
  • Compliance: Avoid assigning employees on holidays or during their leave days.
